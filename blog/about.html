About Me

Students subject to dismissal for academic reason will be dismissed unless placed on probation. No student subject to dismissal may expect probation as a matter of right.


Dismissal with Readmission status is not a right of student rather it is a privilege given to a student by the Academic commission. It is subject to the availability of facilities and the necessary budgetary allocation.
